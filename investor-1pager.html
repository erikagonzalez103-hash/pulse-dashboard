Single-page summary · $175k seed bridge ask

Pulse is the momentum layer for cities. Real-time discovery for what's accelerating, neighborhood by neighborhood. Launching in Dallas, July 14, 2026.

The Problem

Yelp captures reputation. Instagram captures attention. Google captures intent. None of them tell you what's actually happening right now. Discovery tools today are static. Reviews are 18 months old. Algorithms reward engagement, not relevance. Locals work around it. Visitors get default-routed to the same five places. Vendors with real momentum can't surface above incumbents. There's a layer missing.

The Solution

Pulse measures velocity, not volume. We aggregate signals across check-ins, social mentions, time-of-day energy, and vendor-supplied real-time data to produce a live momentum score for every business and neighborhood. Score rings transition from cool to warm to fire as energy accumulates. Consumers get a live discovery surface; vendors get a dashboard, an offer engine, and the ability to boost in surge moments.

Why Now · Why Dallas

The FIFA World Cup is landing in Dallas this summer with nine matches at AT&T Stadium and the semifinal on July 14. 250k+ visitors over five weeks, all making 24-hour decisions, all cut off from local reputation context. Reputation tools fail this user. Momentum tools win them. We launch July 14 with founding vendors locked, infrastructure tested, and a city primed by the most expensive sports tourism event of the decade.
